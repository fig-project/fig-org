The FiG Philosophy

The FiG philosophy is: anything that can be named is alive. The word FiG is the generic term for such a living creature, coined in 1998. So, anything that can be named is a FiG. You are a FiG, objects are FiGs, ideas are FiGs, the FiG philosophy is a FiG, the universe is a FiG.
