Mon, 2007-10-29 21:57 — michael

Why software? Well, software seems to bridge the gap between philosophy and natural science. If something can be implemented in software, then it has a chance to change the physical world without the intervention of humans. Having said that, there are no second-class citizens in the FiG Project; when there are other species of FiGs who want to join the FiG Project publically, we will create other areas of this site to highlight them.

The following are software members of the FiG Project. If you would like to add your Free Software to this list, contact <project@fig.org>:
